About MBK Electronics

I'm a first-year Electrical and Electronic Engineering student using MBK Electronics as a personal brand for my projects and learning journey. My current focus areas include basic circuits, microcontrollers (Arduino/ESP32), KiCad PCB design, and simple repairs.

  • Build real-world electronics skills while I study.
  • Help students and hobbyists bring small electronics ideas to life.
  • Develop a portfolio of working projects and prototypes.

What I Can Help With

Project & Prototype Support

Help with simple circuits, breadboard prototypes, and basic microcontroller projects for students and hobbyists.

Basic Repairs & Diagnostics

Initial checks and small fixes for simple electronic devices (e.g. power issues, connectors, basic soldering).

Learning & Documentation

Clear explanations, diagrams, and beginner-friendly notes as I learn – ideal for students who want to understand the basics.

MBK Electronics is run by a student engineer. I focus on small-scale, educational and prototype work only.
